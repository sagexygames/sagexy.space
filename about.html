About

Frutiger Aero serves as a directory of communities and a guide for the aesthetic/art style of the same name. This site was made by Solinus and kantraa. Contact us if you see any issues or have suggestions for the site.

This was made mostly as a one-time project based on a few tutorials Solinus posted, and as such, will be updated once in a blue moon.
